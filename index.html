How it works

Your 12 weeks, mapped out

Each cohort is based on my '8 gears model' - so you always know what you're working on, and why.

  1. Weeks 1–2

    Get clear on your offer & message

    Pin down who you're for, the problem you solve, and how to say it so the right people lean in. We sharpen your positioning before we touch outreach.

  2. Weeks 3–4

    Build a pipeline you can see

    Map where your best clients actually come from and set up a simple, repeatable way to track opportunities — no bloated CRM required.

  3. Weeks 5–6

    Outreach that doesn't feel icky

    Craft conversations and follow-ups that feel like you. We practise the awkward bits together so they stop being awkward.

  4. Weeks 7–8

    Win the work - pitch & proposals

    Turn interest into signed clients. Bring a live pitch or proposal to the hot seat and get it pressure-tested by the room.

  5. Weeks 9–10

    Make it consistent

    Build the weekly habits that keep your pipeline full, so finding clients stops being a panic and becomes a routine.

  6. Weeks 11–12

    Your 90-day growth plan

    Leave with a clear, written plan for the next quarter and a group that keeps you accountable to it.
